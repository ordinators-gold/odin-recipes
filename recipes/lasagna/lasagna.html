Cheese Filling:

  • 15oz of ricotta cheese ~ 2 cups
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups of mozarella cheese
  • 3/4 cups of parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 2 teaspoons of italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of pepper

Meat Sauce:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
  • 3/4 lbs ground beef
  • 3/4 lbs italian sausage
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 40oz. of marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of hotsauce
  • 1 teaspoon of worcestershire sauce

Lasagna Noodles/Cheese Topping:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups of mozarella cheese

Prep Work:

  1. Combine the Cheese Filling ingredients in a medium bowl and set aside. Measure the remaining ingredients.

Make the Meat Sauce:

  1. Heat olive oil over medium heat and add the diced onions. Let them soften for 10 minutes.
  2. Add the ground beef and sausage and increase heat to medium-high. Use a spatula to break up the meat so that it's fine and crumbled. Cook for 8-10 minutes. Add garlic during the last minute. Dran excess grease.
  3. Add the chicken broth and use a silicon spatula to "clean" the bottom and sides of the pot. Add the marinara sauce, tomato paste, hot sauce, and worcestershire sauce.Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Cover partially and let the sauce simmer gently while you boil the pasta noodles.

Preheat Oven and Cook the Lasagna Noodles:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F
  2. Begin boiling a large pot of salted pasta water for the lasagna noodles. Once a rapid boil is reached, cook the noodles al dente according to package instructions. (set a timer to ensure you don't overcook them). Gently stir with a wooden spoon throughout cooking to prevent the noodles from sticking. Drain and rinse with cold water until noodles are completely cool.
  3. Lay the cooled noodles flat on wax or parchment paper while you begin assembling the lasagna.

Assemble:

  1. Spread 1 heaping cup of of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9 x 13 inch casserole dish. Next, add 4 lasagna noodles, overlapping them if needed.
  2. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by about 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce.
  3. Top with 4 more lasagna noodles, 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture, 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce.
  4. Finish with 4 more lasagna noodles, the rest of the ricotta mixture, and the rest of the meat sauce.
  5. Top with 2 1/2 cups of mozarella cheese.
  6. Spray foil with non-stick cooking spray and plaice it spray-side-down on top of the baking dish to prevent the cheese from sticking to it.

Bake:

  1. Bake for 35 minutes. Remove cover and bake for 10 more minutes
  2. Remove and garnish with fresh parsley. Let it for 15 minutes prior to serving to allow the layers set. Serve with garlic bread with cheese.
